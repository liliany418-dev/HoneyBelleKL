Size and Measurement

Item Features

Size varies according to the product as well as size notation (example S / M size etc), so please be careful.

Ordering Process

Step ① SHOP

Shop your favorite items by selecting the button "add to cart".

Step ② FILL YOUR INFORMATION

When ready, fill the form with your personal information and delivery information.

※Please enter your shipping name and address in English.

Step ③ SELECT PAYMENT/SHIPPING METHOD

Select your payment and shipping method.

The shipping costs depend on the shipping method and weight/volume.

The total shipping fees can be checked at this step.

Step ④ CHECK YOUR ORDER DETAILS

Check each items ordered from your cart (Size, Color etc...).

Make sure every item in your cart is correct and then confirm your order.

Step ⑤ PROCEED WITH THE PAYMENT

Proceed with the payment of your order through the URL link sent by e-mail or through the pop-up screen page.

Step ⑥ NEW ORDER SUCCESSFULLY PLACED

Everything went well♡ Thank you for your new order.

An automatic email will be sent with the resume of your order.

Your order will now be carefully packed and shipped within 3 business days (weekends and national holidays excluded).

**Please kindly check your spam/junk emails if you do not receive it within a day.

We kindly ask for your patience♥

NOTE

When an order is placed no change can be made.

All orders placed are definitive.

As well, NO cancelation, NO shipping method change, NO item exchange and NO item color change are allowed.

Please make sure to check all your items from your order before proceeding with your final payment.

* To ensure that each and every one of our customers can purchase our products with peace of mind, we do not permit the purchase of large quantities of our products for resale or commercial purposes.

We reserve the right to cancel orders for resale or commercial purposes.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.

Delivery

Orders are usually shipped within 3 business days (excluding Saturdays, Sundays, and national holidays).

EMS, DHL and FedEx are available as shipping services.

*Some shipping services are not available for certain countries and regions.

Depending on your country or zone, some shipping services may not be available.

Concerning the shipping fees, they are calculated considering the weight and the size of your ordered items and the packing material.

*Please note that your items are kept only after we have received your payment.

Some of the items may be out of stock before your payment.

*Please enter your shipping name and address in English.

Please avoid entering characters such as "/ · " or similar symbols.

About additional fees such as customs

When sending packages overseas, various expenses such as custom fees may occur when importing in the country of the addressee.

With regard to various expenses such as customs, it is buyers' responsibility to pay for these fees.

Policies on those taxes vary in each country.

Please note, we do not accept any under value request nor write gift on our packages.

Please kindly contact your local custom office for more details.

Long-Term Absence or Delivery Refusal

If the delivery gets rejected due to long-term absence, delivery refusal, or in case of repeated returns of items on customer's own reasons, please understand that we could potentially refuse the future orders from that customer.

Returns / Cancellations

In the unlikely event that you receive a defective or incorrectly shipped item, please contact our customer service within 7 days of receiving the item and follow the instructions.

After confirming the details of the defect, we will replace the item or refund your money.

We appreciate your understanding in this matter.

Return/exchange refusals

For the customer's own reason (size does not fit, color is different from the image, etc.)

Returns without a return/exchange email or inquiry within 7 days of receipt

Discounted items

If the return is not received within 2 weeks after the return notification

Items that have been used once or washed

Items without invoice or tags

Items that have been stained, scratched, or repaired by the customer

Items that are in a significantly different condition at the time of return from the time of delivery (including box and accessories)

Cosmetics and consumables

Items that come in direct contact with bare skin, such as underwear and swimsuits

If an order is returned to us due to non-delivery, including but not limited to absence, incorrect or incomplete address, or refusal to accept the package, the following will apply once the package is returned to our warehouse:

  • Round-trip shipping costs
  • Any customs duties, taxes, or other fees incurred in the destination country

The total of the above costs will be deducted from the original payment amount, and the remaining balance will be refunded to the original payment method.
